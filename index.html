Manuport Logistics reduces 16% CO2 for Affilips

Affilips NV, a global producer of pre-alloys, asked Manuport Logistics for help to reduce their carbon footprint. Manuport Logistics has the versatility to make this happen, and therefore Affilips now uses a combination of barge and road for its transport from Tienen to Antwerp. As a result, the company has saved 17,000 km of road transport and reduced its CO2 emissions by 16%.
